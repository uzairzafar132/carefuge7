Our Services

Based in the heart of Birmingham, we offer a variety of accommodation options – from self-contained flats to large shared homes.

Safe & Quality Housing

We provide fully compliant accommodation, from self-contained flats to shared homes with ensuite rooms, ensuring a secure and comfortable living space.

Personalized Support

Our qualified Housing Support Officers offer weekly one-to-one support, helping you navigate mental health recovery, financial challenges, and more.

Advocacy & Resources

We provide advocacy, advice, and access to education, training, and community resources, empowering you to rebuild your life with confidence.

Why Choose Carefuge Ltd?

We provide high-quality, compliant housing with personalized support to help you regain independence and stability.
  • High-quality, compliant accommodation tailored to your circumstances
  • Weekly personalized support from experienced professionals
  • A focus on independence, choice, and empowerment
  • Housing options for all, from private flats to shared homes
Explore Housing

As a leading provider of accommodation for vulnerable individuals in England, Carefuge specializes in offering temporary housing solutions for homeless adults, those in need of social services, and individuals escaping domestic violence. Our focus is on providing a safe, supportive environment where individuals can regain stability and work towards a brighter future. We are committed to offering not only shelter but the care and resources needed to help our tenants rebuild their lives.
