About us

Company Overview

Carefuge Ltd

Carefuge Ltd is a trusted accommodation provider in Birmingham, specialising in exempt accommodation for homeless individuals aged 18 and over who are on benefits and in need of housing and support. We cater to a diverse range of needs, including mental health challenges, alcohol recovery, drug rehabilitation, domestic violence, and debt struggles. Our goal is to provide not just a safe place to live, but a foundation for healing, growth, and independence..

If you’re on benefits, in need of housing, and facing life’s challenges, Carefuge Ltd is here to help you turn a house into a home.

What is Exempt Accommodation?

Exempt accommodation provides housing alongside essential care and support for those who need more than just a place to stay. At Carefuge Ltd, we use this model to offer enhanced services to vulnerable individuals, ensuring you have both a secure home and the tools to thrive.

Finding the right shelter is more than just a place to stay—it’s about creating a safe, supportive environment that feels like home. At Carefuge, we understand that every individual’s needs are unique. Whether you’re seeking temporary housing, a stable refuge, or a fresh start, our team carefully selects accommodations that align with your specific needs and circumstances. We make the process easier by offering personalized recommendations, expert guidance, and ongoing support every step of the way.
